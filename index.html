    INTEL PC

    Intel PCs are computers that use processors made by Intel Corporation, a leading manufacturer of computer components. The term encompasses a wide range of devices, from budget-friendly desktops to high-end gaming desktops and powerful workstations.

    Ryzen PCs are computers that use processors made by AMD, specifically their Ryzen line of CPUs. These processors are known for their performance, affordability, and multi-core capabilities, making them popular choices for various users like gamers, creators, and professionals.
